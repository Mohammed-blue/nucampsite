Balanced Blood Sugar Smoothie

This is a great smoothie with low-glycemic fruit, so those who have diabetes or otherwise watching their blood sugar levels can still enjoy it without worry.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Greens, Spring Mix
  • 1/2 Banana
  • 1/2 Pear, cored
  • 1 tsp Oats, rolled, gluten-free
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cup Almond Milk, unsweetened
